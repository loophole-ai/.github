## About Loophole

We built Loophole because modern IDEs weren't keeping up with how developers actually think — jumping between context, reasoning through bugs, and planning ahead while writing code.

**Loophole is an AI IDE that thinks while you code.** It's not just autocomplete. Loophole reasons about your codebase in real time, understands your intent, and surfaces insights before you even ask — so you spend less time context-switching and more time building.

Built on top of VS Code, Loophole feels familiar from day one while being powered by AI under the hood.
